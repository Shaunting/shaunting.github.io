NBA Referees

Could NBA referees be influencing the outcome of games? Scott Foster, an experienced NBA referee, is a figure who evokes both respect and controversy. With a history of officiating multiple NBA Finals games, Foster has gained the moniker "The Extender" due to a prevailing theory that he might intentionally make calls that benefit teams on the verge of elimination, thus prolonging playoff series. Foster's zealous enforcement of the game's rules, often without hesitation, has fueled speculation regarding the NBA's ulterior motive of generating higher revenue and viewer engagement through extended series.

A visualization has been created to determine if referees are consistently making an unusually high or low number of calls, suggesting a potential manipulation of game outcomes.

Analyzing Vehicle Fuel Efficiency

During this project, I took on the challenge of unraveling the key factors that influence a vehicle's miles per gallon (MPG). My focus was on four critical continuous variables: volume per cubic space, top speed, horsepower, and vehicular weight.

This journey began with Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA), where I used visualization techniques to spot patterns and fine-tune the data. I then dived into Multilinear Regression models to quantify how each of these variables impacted MPG. Additionally, I performed an ANOVA test to measure the statistical significance and strength of these predictive factors.

This project allowed me to gain valuable insights into the intricate connections between vehicle specifications and fuel efficiency. It was a rewarding experience that provided meaningful contributions to the automotive industry's understanding of fuel efficiency dynamics.

It covers essential steps such as data examination, preparation, model training, and performance testing. I also demonstrate effective data visualization techniques using Matplotlib, feature extraction from time series data, and essential data splitting and normalization practices. The project showcases my mastery in creating and training four distinct time series models: SARIMAX, Facebook Prophet, neural networks, and XGBOOST, offering valuable insights into time series analysis techniques.
